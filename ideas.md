<idea>
**Design Movement**: Modern Mediterranean Industrial
**Core Principles**: Reliability, Warmth, Technical Precision, Local Connection
**Color Philosophy**:
- Primary: **Deep Solar Orange** (#FF9900) - Represents the Algarve sun and energy warmth.
- Secondary: **Slate Charcoal** (#2D3748) - Grounds the design with industrial reliability (HVAC equipment).
- Background: **Warm Sand/Off-White** (#F7F5F0) - Evokes the local architecture and sandy beaches, reducing eye strain compared to stark white.
- Accent: **Teal Blue** (#319795) - Represents cooling/air conditioning, providing a refreshing contrast to the orange.
**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric Split-Screen & Card-Based Grid.
- Avoids standard centered "hero" blocks.
- Uses split layouts (text left, image right) with overlapping elements to create depth.
- Service pillars (AC, Solar, Heat Pumps) presented in a masonry-style grid rather than uniform rows.
**Signature Elements**:
- **Solar Geometric Patterns**: Subtle background SVG patterns resembling solar arrays or airflow curves.
- **"Glassmorphism" Cards**: Service cards with slight transparency and blur, sitting on top of scenic Algarve backgrounds.
- **Rounded-Square Buttons**: Moving away from pill shapes to slightly more structured, engineered feel.
**Interaction Philosophy**: "Confident & Direct". Hover effects are snappy (scale up slightly), buttons have a solid fill transition. No "bouncy" or overly playful animations; focused on professional trust.
**Animation**:
- Entrance: Elements slide in from bottom with a slight fade (0.4s ease-out).
- Scroll: Parallax effect on background images of Algarve landscapes.
**Typography System**:
- Headings: **Outfit** (Geometric sans-serif, modern, approachable). Bold weights for impact.
- Body: **Inter** (Clean, highly readable for technical specs).
</idea>
